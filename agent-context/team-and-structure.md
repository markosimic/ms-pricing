## Role Definitions

### MS Commercial Lead
End-to-end ownership of the deal — contract, commercials, SLAs, and bid governance. Single-threaded owner: when ownership is unclear in a deal, default to MS Commercial Lead. Accountable for end-to-end coherence across the deal, including the integration point when commercial and operational threads run in parallel.

### MS Operations Lead
Owns the operational side of MS delivery. Responsible for day-to-day service operations, team performance, and continuous improvement across engagements.

### Service Management Lead
Oversees the Service Manager team. Responsible for service management quality and consistency across client engagements.

### Service Manager (SM)
The operational lead for a specific client engagement. Key responsibilities:
- Primary point of contact for the client
- SLA compliance management
- Stakeholder communication (client and internal)
- Incident coordination
- Change governance
- Service quality reporting to client and Steering Committee
- Bridges the gap between Zühlke's support team and the client's business units

One dedicated Service Manager (SPOC) is assigned per engagement.

### Service Engineering Team (Support Engineers)
A globally managed team of support engineers. Responsibilities include:
- Application incident response and resolution
- Monitoring and maintenance
- Runbook execution
- Change implementation
- 24×7 coverage where contracted

### Account Lead
Owns the client relationship and is the primary escalation point. Responsible for deal entry. Not the day-to-day operational contact — that is the Service Manager.

### Delivery Lead
Manages handover from project to MS. Works with Service Manager during transition. Involved in the Operations Readiness Checklist sign-off.

### Legal
Must be involved early in contract review and approval — not at the end.

## Team Assembly Model

The MS team is **not a fixed unit**. It is assembled per engagement based on service scope and complexity. A dedicated SPOC (Service Manager) is assigned to each Engagement Lead so coordination has a single entry point.

Staffing includes both onshore (CH/DE) and Global Delivery Center (GDC) resources. Language requirements (especially German for DACH clients) affect staffing options.

## Governance Structure per Engagement

```
Client ←→ Service Manager ←→ Service Engineering Team
              ↕
       Steering Committee
              ↕
     MS Commercial Lead / MS Operations Lead
```

Key governance touchpoints:
- **Steering Committee**: formal oversight body for the engagement
- **QBR (Quarterly Business Review)**: regular structured review with client on service performance, trends, and improvement plans
- **Monthly reporting**: standard deliverable; custom reports possible but increase cost

## RACI — Proposal & Commercial Process

| Activity | MS Commercial Lead | Service Manager | Account Lead | Delivery Lead |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Receive client request | I | I | R/A | I |
| Pass to MS Commercial Lead | A | I | R | I |
