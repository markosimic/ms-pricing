## What Is This?

**Zühlke Managed Services (MS)** is a business unit within the Zühlke Group. It provides ongoing, outcome-based management of clients' critical digital applications — keeping them running, secure, and improving after go-live.

This is not the same as Zühlke's core project delivery business. Project delivery = sell engineering effort. Managed Services = sell a recurring service with guaranteed outcomes. The pricing model is subscription-based (monthly/quarterly). The contractual commitment is defined SLAs, not a project scope.

**Strategic importance:** MS is a key pillar of Zühlke's "Innovate, Build, and Operate" strategy (2025–2027), driving the shift from one-time project revenue to recurring, scalable revenue.

---

## Two Core Services

- **AMS (Application Management Services)** — monitoring, maintenance, incident response, and support for live applications. Takes over when development ends.
- **CMS (Cloud Managed Services)** — cloud infrastructure operations, starting early in the product lifecycle.

## How Deals Work (3 Phases)

1. **Bid** — Gate 0/1 must be cleared before contracting. MS Commercial Lead owns the deal end-to-end. Initial Questions and Business Criticality Assessment drive the SLA tier and pricing.
2. **Contract** — Use approved modular templates from SharePoint. German contract for DACH. No custom SLA combos. Avoid penalty-only clauses.
3. **Onboarding** — Transition checklist, operations readiness sign-off, ITSM tool setup, kick-offs with engineers and client.

---

## Key Rules to Know

- **Gate 0/1 is mandatory** — no contracting without them
- **MS Commercial Lead = single-threaded owner** for all commercial/contract questions
- **Zühlke owns the service outcome** — not just the capacity (this is not staff augmentation)
- **ISO 20000 processes cannot be bypassed** — Zühlke is certified and audited
- **DACH contracts stay in German** — use the approved template, never translate or adapt it
- **Penalty-only clauses → always counter with bonus/malus**
