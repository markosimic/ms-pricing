## Client Industry Profile

The portfolio is heavily concentrated in:
- **Banking / Financial Services**: SGKB, SHKB, Zurich Insurance, SWICA
- **Public Sector / Justice**: Justitia 4.0, Stadt Zürich
- **Healthcare**: USZ, Helsana, Erbe Elektromedizin, SWICA
- **Infrastructure / Industrial**: SwissGrid, Zürich Airport, SR Technics, Thyssenkrupp
- **Real Estate / Other**: VIMOVA, KKL, MS Direct

This concentration in regulated industries makes ISO 20000 certification a key commercial differentiator — these clients often require or strongly prefer certified providers.

## Geographic Profile

- Primarily Switzerland (CH), with some DE-based engagements
- DACH-focused
- Most client-facing deliverables are in German
- Some projects with non-German-speaking team members — Language Plans document expectations

## Key Client Contact Patterns

Each engagement has:
- A **client-side contact** (Service/Application Manager or equivalent) for day-to-day communication
- A **Steering Committee** for formal governance (typically senior stakeholder level)
- Links to **Vertec** for commercial/time tracking reference
